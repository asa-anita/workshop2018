Gravitational wave astronomy school program

The school will take place over three days, two at UWA (Mon, Tues) and the third at Gingin (Wed). Please note that there is limited space available for the trip to Gingin so register your interest early.
There will be a day on GW analysis, a day on LIGO astrophysics split into two half sessions (GW astrophysics & multimessenger), and a half day on LIGO instrumentation. Speakers (TBC) are
  • Kendall Ackley (Monash): Multimessenger Astronomy
  • Jade Powell (Swinburne): Astrophysics of LIGO sources
  • Stefan Oslowski (Swinburne): Pulsar Timing Arrays
  • Rory Smith (Monash): LIGO Data Analysis and Parameter Inference
  • Rob Ward (ANU): Instrumentation
